# Difficulty Rules

Difficulty should be more than word length.

Good difficulty dimensions:
- word length
- letter frequency
- phonics complexity
- word family familiarity
- whether the learner has recently missed similar words
- whether the player is in a strong or weak session

Rules:
- do not equate difficulty with frustration
- do not jump too fast
- do not punish mistakes
- make hard words feel achievable through sequencing
